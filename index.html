About Trinity

Journalist.
Creative.
Visionary.

I'm Trinity Alana — a Howard University-trained broadcast journalist, on-camera host, and founder of Notoriety Creative Agency. Currently a News Associate at CNN, I've reported across print, broadcast, and audio, covering everything from Capitol Hill to culture festivals.

I sit at the intersection of Black creativity, civic life, and pop culture — and I tell those stories with precision and heart. Whether I'm behind a camera, on a stage, or producing a campaign, I bring the same commitment to craft and community.

As founder of Notoriety Creative Agency, I build the pipeline between brands and the creatives who make culture move — producing campaigns, events, and content that create real community connection in the DMV and beyond.
